I’m Holly Landrum-Mullen, a Purdue University Graduate. I have been in Residential Design and Construction since 1986. I started my career doing drafting work for a local residential designer before being hired by one of the nation’s leading production builders to design their plans for them. In 1996, I decided to step out and start my own business, “h.r. design”.

Ever since I was a little girl, I have had a passion for architecture. I love the challenge of creating a plan that fits the individual needs of my client. I listen to their wants and needs when putting together a house plan that fits their lifestyle while keeping their budget in mind.

I start the process with a free consultation to discuss wants, desires, and needs. From there, I put together a set of preliminary plans that gives the client a floor plan with basic room dimensions and a front elevation of the house. With my Autocad program, the client can also see their plan in 3-D. Once the preliminaries are “nailed” down, I go to construction plans. These give the builder what they need for the construction process.
